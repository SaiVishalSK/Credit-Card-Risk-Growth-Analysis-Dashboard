**Project Overview**

This project analyzes a credit card customer portfolio to identify risk exposure and growth opportunities using credit utilization behavior. The dashboard helps balance upsell potential with credit risk control through KPI-driven and segment-level analysis.

**Business Objectives**

1) Identify upsell-ready customers with low credit utilization

2) Monitor high-risk and medium-risk customers

3) Understand customer behavior across income bands and card categories

4) Support data-driven credit expansion and risk mitigation
